# Overview - Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

This project analyzes six months of coffee shop transactional sales data to evaluate revenue performance, customer purchasing behavior, product popularity, store performance, and sales trends.

The project was built entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, Slicers, and an interactive dashboard**. The objective was to transform raw transactional sales data into meaningful business insights that support operational efficiency and data-driven decision-making.

---

# Business Problem

Coffee shops process thousands of customer transactions every day, generating large volumes of sales data. Without proper analysis, identifying customer purchasing patterns, evaluating store performance, monitoring sales trends, and recognizing top-performing products becomes difficult.

This project aims to:

- Analyze monthly sales performance and revenue trends
- Identify peak business hours with the highest customer activity
- Evaluate sales performance across different store locations
- Identify the highest revenue-generating product categories and product types
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior and order trends
- Build an interactive dashboard for business monitoring
- Generate actionable recommendations to improve operational efficiency and profitability

# Key Findings

- The coffee shop generated **$698,812.33** in total sales from **149,116 customer orders** over six months.
- Average order value was **$4.69**, with customers purchasing an average of **1.44 items per transaction**.
- Customer demand peaked between **8:00 AM and 10:00 AM**, making it the busiest sales period.
- **Coffee** was the highest revenue-generating category, contributing **39%** of total revenue.
- **Barista Espresso** emerged as the best-selling product by revenue.
- **Hell's Kitchen** recorded the highest revenue among all store locations, although all branches showed balanced performance.
- Large-sized beverages generated the highest revenue, while a significant percentage of transactions contained undefined size values, highlighting opportunities to improve data quality.
- Customer demand remained consistent throughout the week, with **Friday** recording the highest number of orders.

# Final Recommendations

- Schedule additional staff during the **8:00 AM–10:00 AM** peak business hours to improve service efficiency.
- Continue promoting high-performing products such as **Barista Espresso** through featured menu placement and seasonal campaigns.
- Introduce bundled offers combining coffee with bakery items to increase average transaction value.
- Improve point-of-sale data entry by ensuring product sizes are consistently recorded.
- Monitor store performance regularly and replicate successful operational practices across all branches.
- Implement customer loyalty programs to encourage repeat purchases and increase customer retention.
- Expand premium coffee offerings and introduce seasonal beverages to strengthen revenue growth.
