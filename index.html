Project Overview

The Coffee Shop Sales Analysis project explores sales performance, customer purchasing behaviour, product demand, and operational trends using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence through professional reporting and interactive visualizations, enabling stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions.

Project Highlights

☕ Sales Performance Analysis

Analyze overall revenue performance, monthly sales trends, transaction volumes, and business growth to understand how the coffee shop performs over time.

📦 Product Performance

Identify best-selling products, category contribution, and purchasing patterns to understand customer preferences and optimize product offerings.

👥 Customer Behaviour

Examine customer purchasing habits, order frequency, and peak business periods to improve customer experience and operational planning.

📈 Business Intelligence

Convert raw sales data into meaningful insights using interactive reporting, helping decision-makers identify opportunities for growth and improved profitability.

🎯 Executive Reporting

Present business findings through a clean executive-style report containing KPIs, trends, recommendations, and actionable insights for business stakeholders.

💡 Data-Driven Decisions

Support strategic decision-making by highlighting key performance indicators, customer trends, sales opportunities, and operational improvements.

Tools & Technologies

📊 Microsoft Excel

Primary platform used for data cleaning, analysis, dashboard development, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and business reporting.

📈 Pivot Tables & Charts

Used to summarize large datasets, calculate business metrics, identify trends, and create interactive visualizations.

🎛 Interactive Slicers

Enabled dynamic filtering of sales data, allowing users to explore insights across multiple business dimensions.

🧹 Data Cleaning

Prepared and organized raw transactional data to ensure accuracy and consistency before performing business analysis.

📄 Executive Business Reporting

Created a professional business report summarizing analytical findings, strategic recommendations, and executive insights.

🌐 GitHub Pages

Used to publish and host the project report online, making it easily accessible for recruiters, hiring managers, and portfolio visitors.

Business Highlights

📊 Revenue Trends

Understand how sales fluctuate across different time periods and identify growth opportunities based on business performance.

🕒 Peak Business Hours

Identify the busiest hours and days to optimize staffing, inventory planning, and customer service.

🥤 Product Insights

Discover top-performing products and categories that generate the highest contribution to overall sales.

📍 Business Recommendations

Generate actionable recommendations that help improve operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and long-term business growth.
